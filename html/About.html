For more than 60 years, Western has been supplying products for the control, storage, and transmission of high-pressure gases to the industrial, medical, and specialty gas markets–and leading the field in the ways that matter most. Western is dedicated to being the best provider of compressed gas systems and components.

Industrial processors and fabricators have made Western a leading source of high-quality compressed gas products used for the control, storage, and transmission of high-pressure gases. Worldwide distribution, coupled with a continuous flow of innovative new products, supports Western’s reputation as a veteran and pacesetting supplier.
